General School Safety Measures

 Access Control:
We maintain a controlled access system, ensuring that only authorized individuals can enter the premises. Visitors are required to sign in at the main office and wear visitor badges for easy identification.

Emergency Drills:
Regular fire drills, lockdown drills, and evacuation drills are conducted to ensure everyone is familiar with the procedures in case of emergencies.

Security Personnel:
Trained security personnel are stationed throughout the campus to monitor activities and respond swiftly to any security concerns.

CCTV Surveillance:
We have a comprehensive network of CCTV cameras placed strategically across the campus to enhance security monitoring.

Student Safety

 Bullying Prevention:
We have a strict anti-bullying policy in place. Students are educated about the importance of kindness and respect, and a dedicated team is available to address any bullying incidents.

Safe Transportation:
If your child uses our transportation services, rest assured that our buses are equipped with safety features and driven by trained drivers who prioritize student safety.

Medical Care:
Our school nurse's office is well-equipped to provide immediate medical attention in case of minor injuries or illnesses. We also maintain records of any allergies or medical conditions.

Emergency Preparedness:

 Communication:
In case of emergencies, we have a well-established communication system to notify parents, guardians, and staff promptly.

Emergency Contacts:
Please ensure your emergency contact information is up to date to facilitate efficient communication during any unforeseen situations.

Collaboration with Authorities:

 We work closely with local law enforcement and emergency services to ensure a coordinated response in case of serious incidents.

Safety Education

 Safety Curriculum:
Our students receive age-appropriate safety education, empowering them with knowledge about personal safety, online safety, and general emergency procedures.

Parent Involvement:
We encourage parents and guardians to engage in safety discussions with their children at home, reinforcing the importance of staying safe both in and out of school.
